# Kolektibo — Documentation Index
_Created: 2026-07-11 01:46 MPST · Kolektibo · Stellar Testnet_

**Kolektibo — AI-Governed Group Treasury on Stellar.** An AI treasurer for any pooled fund
(barangay project funds, church collections, co-op capital, class/reunion funds, team
_pondohan_), made trustworthy by a Soroban smart contract: the AI does a treasurer's labor,
but the money lives in a contract that only releases funds when the group's own on-chain
policy is satisfied. No single officer — and not the AI — can drain the fund.

Built for the **APAC Stellar Hackathon 2026** (final submission Jul 15, Demo Day Jul 18).
